Why Niraamaya?

In the times we live in, most of the illness is caused by the bad lifestyle. Of course, medicines will give relief from the illness but the real solution would be correcting the flaws in the lifestyle.

At Niraamaya Ayurveda, we believe in treating the patients by finding the root cause of the illness rather than treating the symptoms with the help of medicines. This approach of treatment will help the patient to recover gradually from the illness and most importantly, it improves the overall health of the individual.

Dr. Nalini Marath

Graduated from Government Ayurvedic Medical College - Bangalore in 2011, Dr. Nalini Marath has worked her way up in various ayurvedic hospitals. Over a decade-long practice, she has brought relief to thousands of patients and thousands of smiles to count on. The key to the successful treatment of her patients lies in the method she chose - Classical Ayurveda. Over the decade, she has mastered the art of balancing the Doshas of the human body by correcting the lifestyle and administering the right medicines, which gave her patients not just relief but also improved the overall health in long run.
